Hutech University

Ho Chi Minh City University of Technology - HUTECH is a private university. The school officially changed from people-founded to private according to Decision No. 702/QD - TTg of the Prime Minister since May 19, 2010. Currently, the HUTECH education system (HUTECH Education) includes 2 member universities: Ho Chi Minh City University of Technology (HUTECH) and University of Economics and Finance (UEF), besides , there is also Royal Bilingual Inter-Level School - Royal Bilingual International School - Royal School.
